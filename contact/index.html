About Our Online Coaching Program

ByteCraft Data Lab provides comprehensive online coaching for Python programming, SQL database management, and Data Analytics fundamentals. Our classes are specifically designed for students and working professionals in Latur, Maharashtra, and surrounding regions who want to develop practical programming skills.

We conduct live interactive sessions every evening via Microsoft Teams, making it convenient for college students and working professionals to attend from anywhere in Maharashtra. Our 8-week structured curriculum covers Python from basics to intermediate level, essential SQL queries and database operations, and foundational data analytics concepts.

The evening batch runs Monday through Friday from 8:00 PM to 9:30 PM, allowing participants to balance their regular schedules with skill development. Each session includes hands-on coding practice, real-world examples, and weekly projects to reinforce learning. Our instructor, based in Latur, brings academic expertise and practical teaching experience to help students build a strong programming foundation.

Whether you are an Engineering student, BCA student, Polytechnic student, or a professional looking to upskill, our online Python and SQL classes provide the flexibility and structure needed for effective learning. We keep batch sizes limited to ensure personalized attention during live sessions.
